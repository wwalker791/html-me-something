A Beginner's Guide to Monster Hunter

Pick a Weapon That Suits You!

There are 14 types of weapons in the world of Monster Hunter. From the quick and agile sword & shield, to the long ranged bow,to the sluggish but immensely powerful great sword. You can go into training mode to give all of them a try and find one that suits your preferred playstyle.

Know Your Prey!

Every monster is different; each with their own weak points, attack patterns and ailments they can inflict. Against a venomous monster, make sure you have plenty of antidotes. If facing a nimble foe, try setting a trap to keep them in place long enought to strike. Being aware of what you're up against can be the difference between victory and defeat.

Upgrade Your Equipment!

Use the parts that you collect from defeating monsters to forge stronger equipment to slay even stronger monsters! Check in with the smithy often to see what's available.

Team Up!

If you're having trouble when you're starting out, try going online and joining a hunting party. The Zenny and materials that you obtain will give you a leg up in the single player mode.

But most importantly, have fun!
